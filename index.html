HackerRank FCDS is a non-profit community within the Faculty of Computers and Data Science dedicated to fostering technical excellence and collaboration among students.
Our mission is to help students excel in software development, and to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands through engaging workshops and collaborative events.

About

In the 5th season of HackerRank marked as "Elite Era", We are trying to fill the gap between the academic knowledge and the job market, Which We will acheive that through:

Events

Both technical and non technical events that help students know more about the job market.

Workshops

Technical workshops that cover crucial topics and their use cases in real-world applications.

Materials

Studying materials for both university courses and self-study courses.

Events & Workshops

Events and workshops are great opportunities to connect, learn, and grow.
From hands-on sessions to insightful talks, they inspire creativity, and spark innovation.

Intro to C Programming Workshop

Mastering the basics of C programming is a worthwhile endeavour. A powerful low language that teaches you the essential concepts like variables, loops, functions, and memory management. Concepts you will need for the rest of your programming career.
Click here to see it.

Object-Oriented Programming Workshop

Diving into object-oriented programming and learning how to design efficient, reusable, and scalable systems is crucial nowadays to ecxel in the job market. The workshop covered the principles of OOP, Designing classes using UML diagrams, and hands-on projects.
Click here to see it.

Machine Learning Workshop

Machine intelligence is the last invention that humanity will ever need to make. This workshop introduced key concepts in ML, such as supervised and unsupervised learning, and provided hands-on experience with tools to build accurate predictive models.
Click here to see it.

Pre-Security Workshop

Security is the bedrock of trust; without it, every connection is a risk. This workshop helped building strong foundation in cybersecurity. The pre-Security workshop covered key concepts in Networks, Linux, Web Security, and how to protect systems from potential vulnerabilities.
Click here to see it.

CTRL+A Event

CTRL+A is our annual event for high school students preparing to join the Faculty of Computers and Data Science at Alexandria university. In CTRL+A, we gather elite students from all academic years to guide and help incoming students in their early days at UNI.
Click here to see it.

Project Engine

Project Engine is an annual workshop for high school students preparing to join the Faculty of Computers and Data Science at Alexandria university. In Project Engine, we help the newcommers understand the basics of computer science through interactive sessions and hands-on projects.
Click here to see it.

Math Fundamentals Workshop

Mathematical foundations are crucial for our Computer Science journey. So this provided the high school students preparing to join the Faculty of Computers and Data Science at Alexandria university with the needed basics in Mathematics.
Click here to see it.

Mastering Problem-Solving Interviews

Joining Elite-Tech companies isn't easy, and requires a lot of preparation. So Eng. Mostafa Mansour, SWE II @Microsoft Copenhagen, was here to help us prepare for technichal interviews with his great experience in FAANG companies.
Click here to watch the session.
